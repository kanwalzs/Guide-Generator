# High-level idea
Template to help users get started with data quality monitoring. Walk you through how to:
*   - Set up roles and objects with the required access for data quality monitoring.
*   - Define a data metric function (DMF) schedule and setup associations and expectations.
*   - Create a custom DMF to measure data quality.



# Feature details & references
Our data quality monitoring and profiling work together to give you complete visibility into your data's health, by automatically analyzing datasets to uncover structure, patterns, and anomalies, then continuously tracking and alerting you to issues before they impact your analytics, AI models, or business decisions. This empowers you to build trust in your data, resolve problems faster, and maintain consistent, reliable insights at scale
